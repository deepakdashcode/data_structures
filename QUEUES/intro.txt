It follows the FIFO ( First In First Out ) principle

It has two ends

(i)  The rear end
(ii) The front end

It has two operations

(i)  enqueue --> add data to the rear end
(ii) dequeue --> delete data from the front end

Here insertion of new elements take place from the back(rear) side and deletion occurs from the front side

Some Terms
...........
(i)   Peek --> Refers to inspecting the value at front of the queue

(ii)  Overflow --> refers to a situation (Error) in which one tries to insert an element to a queue of fixed size
                   with insufficient memory to hold the new element

(iii) Underflow --> refers to a situation (Error) when one tries to delete an element from an empty queue
